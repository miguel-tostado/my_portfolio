About Me

Front-End Web Designer and Developer Based in Maine

My name is Miguel Tostado and my goal is to help your business grow.

I've been interested in web development since high school where I was lucky enough to have a class dedicated to creating and designing websites. It didn't take long for me get hooked!

Shortly after high school I enlisted in the US Navy as a nuclear electrician's mate (EMN2/SS) for 6 years. It was a very rewarding life experience and I pushed myself hard to succeed on my boat. After leaving the Navy, I went back to San Antonio, Texas for school (UTSA) where I majored in physics and computer science.

Combing the discipline I gained with the critical thinking of learning physics and the technical skills from my computer science classes, I know how to do what it takes to succeed. I continue to learn and improve my skills so can provide the best services to my clients.
